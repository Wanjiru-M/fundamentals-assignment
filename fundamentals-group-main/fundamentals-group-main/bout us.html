Who Are We

Nelion safaris is an adventures organization with a mandate to help our clients explore the prime wildlife areas of East Africa. We collaborate with major destinations such as hotels, wildlife parks, and private sandy beaches owners to give you best experience of East Africa travel. Our services are personal, attentive and friendly. Your safety is our concern and we ensure that both health wise and security you are secure in case of need, which is enforced by constant communications and medical backups./style>

Our Values

Nelion safaris has a key objective of our client's satisfaction. We ensure sustainable ecotourism by adhering to the rules of each and every nation based of the wildlife human interaction. We also closely work together with local community to give you the experience of African culture.
